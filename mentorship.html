⌘ Connect

A unique two-month long mentorship program that pairs high school and college students to college students or career professionals in the tech industry based on their future career preferences

Our next cycle is for March to April in 2021.

Be a Mentee

Are you a high school or college student that identifies as female or non-binary?


Join us as a mentee for the chance to learn more about the tech industry, network, and meet more people!

Be a Mentor

Do you have career or internship experience in the tech infudstry? Do you have 2-3 hours a month?


Join us as a mentor to help guide the next generation of changemakers from around the world!

Partner with Command Tech

Are you a part of an organization/club or university/company that has similar goals?


Fill in the form with your contact details, and we will schedule a call to see how we can work together to bring this experience to more people!
